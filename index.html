Our Story

Built on Love for
the Book of Allah

Nisaa'atul Tadabbur was born from a simple desire — to create a space where sisters could gather around the Qur'an, not as a ritual, but as a relationship. A living, breathing connection with the words of Allah.

✦

Rooted in the Qur'an

Every program, reflection, and gathering begins and ends with the Book of Allah.

🌿
